Jackie Hill Perry (nee Jackie Hill, born June 21,1989) is an American poet,
writer and hip-pop artist who initially garnered popularity for her performances of
spoken words pieces such as "my Life as a stud", "A poem about weed", and
"Jig-a-Boo" at the passion for Christ movement (P4CM). she has written for
various christain ministry organizations, such as The Resurgence and John
piper's Desiring God, on the topic of christainity and homosexuality. she signed
to the portland, oregon-baed christain hip hop label Humble Beast in january
2014, and released her debut album on November 4, 2014, available both
commercially and for free. Hill married fellow spoken artist Preston perry in
march 2014; the couple currently have four children.

Hill belives that an encounter with sexual abuse as a child contributed to the
confusion over her gender identity she faced while growing up, and her
discovery at age seventeen that she was a lesbian. she lived a life of sexual
Promiscuity and struggled with drug and pormnography addiction, until her conversion to christainity in 2008. Her self proclaimed status as a former Lesbian
has sparked controversy, with many gay rights activist stating that sexual
orientation cannot be changed and that Jackie Hill Perry's experience lends
credence to the idea of conversion theraphy. Jackie hill Perry maintains her belief
God can tramsform lives and that he empowers believers to resist
temptation. She signed to Reach records on June 13,2024
